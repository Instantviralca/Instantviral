# InstantViral v2

# Document 14.02 — Global Testimonials & Reviews System

Version: 1.0
Status: Production Specification

## Purpose

Create one centralized testimonials and reviews system that can be reused across:

- Homepage
- Service pages
- About page
- Admin panel
- Future Learn pages
- Future customer dashboard

The system must use authentic customer reviews only.

It must prevent fabricated testimonials, unsupported ratings, and incorrect Review or AggregateRating schema.

---

## Objectives

The system should:

- Store genuine reviews in one data source
- Support platform-specific and service-specific reviews
- Support approval and publication status
- Support homepage and service-page placement
- Support review moderation
- Support future admin management
- Generate schema only when valid genuine review data exists
- Hide review sections cleanly when no approved reviews are available

---

## Review Data Model

Each review should support:

- id
- customerName
- customerInitials
- customerAvatar (optional)
- platform
- serviceSlug (optional)
- orderIdReference (internal, optional)
- rating
- title (optional)
- reviewText
- reviewDate
- verifiedPurchase
- source
- status
- featured
- displayLocations
- consentConfirmed
- createdAt
- updatedAt

---

## Supported Statuses

- Draft
- Pending Review
- Approved
- Rejected
- Hidden

Only Approved reviews may appear publicly.

---

## Review Sources

Supported sources may include:

- Direct customer submission
- Support follow-up
- Verified order feedback
- Third-party review platform
- Imported historical review

Do not claim a review is verified unless the verification method is real.

---

## Display Locations

Reviews may be configured for:

- Homepage
- Specific service page
- Platform group
- About page
- Admin preview only

A review may appear in more than one location when relevant.

---

## Rating Rules

Supported rating scale:

1 to 5

Requirements:

- Rating must be an integer or supported decimal according to the final UI
- Public stars must include accessible text
- Rating must not be inferred from sentiment
- Do not invent missing ratings
- Do not average hidden or rejected reviews

---

## Verified Purchase

The verifiedPurchase field should be true only when the review can be matched to a genuine completed order.

Do not display "Verified Purchase" based only on customer claims.

---

## Review Card Structure

Each review card may display:

- Customer name or approved public display name
- Initials or avatar
- Rating
- Review title
- Review text
- Platform or service
- Review date
- Verified Purchase badge when valid

Do not display internal order references publicly.

---

## Homepage Testimonials

Homepage should show:

- 3 approved featured reviews on desktop
- Stacked reviews on mobile

If fewer than 3 genuine reviews exist:

- Show only available approved reviews, or
- Hide the section through configuration

Do not create placeholder reviews that appear genuine.

---

## Service Page Reviews

A service page should prioritize:

1. Reviews linked to that exact service
2. Reviews linked to that platform
3. General InstantViral reviews

If no relevant approved reviews exist, hide the section.

---

## Empty State

Public website:

Hide the review section when no approved reviews are available.

Admin:

Show a clear empty state with actions to add or import reviews.

---

## Review Moderation

Admin review workflow should support:

- View full review
- Approve
- Reject
- Hide
- Mark as featured
- Assign platform
- Assign service
- Confirm consent
- Confirm verified purchase
- Add internal moderation note

Internal moderation notes must never appear publicly.

---

## Consent and Privacy

Before publishing a customer review, confirm that InstantViral has permission to display:

- Customer name or approved public name
- Review text
- Rating
- Avatar, if used
- Service or platform reference

Do not publish personal information beyond what is necessary.

---

## Editing Reviews

Avoid changing the meaning of genuine reviews.

Permitted editing should be limited to:

- Correcting obvious spelling errors
- Removing sensitive information
- Shortening with customer approval
- Formatting for readability

Do not rewrite reviews to make them more positive.

---

## Schema Rules

Review and AggregateRating schema may be generated only when:

- Reviews are genuine
- Reviews are publicly visible
- Ratings are present
- The reviewed entity is clearly defined
- The schema matches the visible page content
- The implementation follows current search-engine structured-data requirements

Do not generate:

- Review schema from hidden reviews
- AggregateRating from placeholders
- Ratings from testimonials without numeric scores
- Service-level ratings from unrelated general reviews

---

## Aggregate Rating Calculation

When enabled, calculate from:

- Approved
- Publicly visible
- Relevant
- Numeric-rated reviews only

Store or compute:

- ratingValue
- reviewCount
- bestRating
- worstRating

Do not manually enter aggregate values that differ from the approved data.

## Search and Filters

Admin architecture should prepare for:

- Search by customer name
- Search by review text
- Filter by platform
- Filter by service
- Filter by rating
- Filter by status
- Filter by verified purchase
- Filter by featured
- Sort by newest, oldest, highest rating, lowest rating

---

## Accessibility

- Star ratings include screen-reader text
- Cards use semantic structure
- Dates are readable
- Verified badges do not rely on colour alone
- Focus states visible
- Carousels should be avoided unless fully accessible
- Prefer grid or stacked layout

---

## Responsive Rules

Desktop:

- 3-column testimonial grid
- Equal-height cards where practical

Tablet:

- 2-column grid

Mobile:

- Single-column stack
- No horizontal carousel required
- Full review text remains readable

---

## Analytics Events

Prepare typed events:

- testimonial_section_view
- testimonial_card_view
- testimonial_service_click
- review_source_click
- review_expand
- review_admin_approve
- review_admin_reject
- review_admin_feature

Do not integrate a vendor unless already configured.

---

## Performance

- Lazy-load avatars below the fold
- Use `next/image` for real avatars
- Avoid heavy sliders
- Render only approved reviews publicly
- Paginate large admin review lists

---

## Security

- Sanitize review text
- Prevent script injection
- Protect internal notes
- Restrict moderation actions to authorized admins
- Log approval and status changes
- Avoid exposing customer email or internal order IDs
