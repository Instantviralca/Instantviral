# InstantViral v2

# Document 14.05 — Internal Linking Engine

Version: 1.0
Status: Production Specification

## Purpose

Build a centralized internal-linking system that automatically creates relevant, maintainable links across the website.

Goals:

- Improve SEO crawlability
- Strengthen topical authority
- Reduce orphan pages
- Keep links consistent
- Avoid hardcoded cross-links

---

## Link Sources

Support links between:

- Homepage
- Service pages
- Platform hubs (future)
- FAQ
- About
- Contact
- Legal pages
- Learn articles (future)

---

## Approved Service Scope

Only use these production services:

Instagram:
- Followers
- Likes
- Views
- Comments

TikTok:
- Followers
- Likes
- Views

Facebook:
- Followers
- Page Likes
- Post Likes

YouTube:
- Subscribers
- Views

Do not generate links to skipped services.

---

## Data Model

Each page supports:

- slug
- title
- category
- platform
- keywords
- relatedServices
- relatedArticles
- relatedPolicies
- breadcrumbParent
- active

---

## Required Helpers

Create:

- getRelatedServices()
- getRelatedPolicies()
- getRelatedArticles()
- buildBreadcrumb()
- validateInternalLinks()
- findOrphanPages()
- findBrokenLinks()
- getRecommendedAnchors()

---

## Related Service Rules

Priority:

1. Same platform
2. Same customer intent
3. Closely related purchase journey

Maximum:

- 3 related services on service pages
- 4 on homepage sections

---

## Policy Links

Where appropriate link to:

- Privacy Policy
- Refund Policy
- Terms & Conditions
- Cookie Policy
- Disclaimer
- FAQ
- Contact

Use contextual anchor text.

---

## Breadcrumb Rules

Examples:

Home > Instagram > Buy Instagram Followers

Home > Facebook > Buy Facebook Page Likes

Home > FAQ

Home > Privacy Policy

Use one BreadcrumbList schema source.

---

## Link Validation

Detect:

- Broken routes
- Inactive pages
- Duplicate links
- Circular references
- Orphan pages

Report issues instead of silently ignoring them.

---

## Anchor Text

Use descriptive anchors.

Avoid:

- Click here
- Read more
- Learn more (without context)

Prefer:

- Buy Instagram Followers
- Refund Policy
- Track Your Order

---

## Accessibility

- Keyboard accessible
- Clear focus states
- Descriptive link text
- No empty anchors
