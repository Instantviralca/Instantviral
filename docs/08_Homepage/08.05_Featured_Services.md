# InstantViral v2

# Document 08.05 — Featured Services

Version: 1.0

Status: Production Specification

---

# Purpose

The Featured Services section is a concise conversion area on the homepage.

It surfaces a curated mix of high-intent services across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook — without duplicating full platform catalogs on the homepage.

Platform hubs and individual service pages do the heavy lifting for depth and SEO.

---

# Homepage Position

Final homepage structure:

1. Hero
2. Platform Selector (4 cards)
3. Why InstantViral
4. How It Works
5. Featured Services (mixed platforms)
6. Trust Signals
7. Statistics
8. Testimonials
9. FAQ
10. Final CTA
11. Footer

---

# Business Goals

- Give quick access to popular services.
- Strengthen internal links to commercial pages.
- Keep the homepage short and focused.
- Avoid repetitive per-platform service strips.

---

# UX Goals

Users should answer:

“Which popular service do I want to explore next?”

Then continue to a service page or a platform hub for more options.

---

# Section Heading

## H2

Featured Growth Services

## Intro

40–60 words. Explain that visitors can explore popular services across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook.

---

# Layout

Desktop: 3 columns  
Tablet: 2 columns  
Mobile: 1 column

---

# Curated Services (8–12)

Example set:

- Buy Instagram Followers
- Buy Instagram Likes
- Buy TikTok Followers
- Buy TikTok Views
- Buy YouTube Subscribers
- Buy YouTube Views
- Buy Facebook Followers
- Buy Facebook Page Likes

All entries must come from the Service Registry.

---

# Card Structure

- Platform badge
- Service name
- Short description (20–30 words)
- Explore CTA → service page (`service.url` from registry)

---

# Data Sources

- `data/services.ts`
- `data/platforms.ts`
- `data/content/homepage.ts` (`servicesGrid`)

Do not hardcode service names or URLs in components.
