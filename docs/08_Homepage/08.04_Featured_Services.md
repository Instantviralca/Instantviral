# InstantViral v2

# Document 08.04 — Featured Services

Version: 1.0

Status: Production Specification

---

# Purpose

The Featured Services section is the primary conversion area of the homepage.

After selecting a platform, users should immediately discover the most popular services available.

The goal is to reduce clicks, shorten the path to purchase, and help users quickly find the service they need.

---

# Business Goals

- Increase service page visits.
- Improve homepage conversion rate.
- Highlight high-demand services.
- Strengthen internal linking.
- Support SEO by linking to commercial pages.

---

# UX Goals

Users should be able to answer:

"Which service do I need?"

The section should feel organized and easy to scan.

---

# Layout

Desktop:

- Responsive grid.
- 3 cards per row.

Tablet:

- 2 cards per row.

Mobile:

- Single-column stack.

---

# Section Heading

## H2

Popular Social Media Services

## Intro

Introduce the section in 40–60 words.

Explain that users can explore services for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook.

---

# Card Structure

Each service card includes:

- Platform badge
- Service name
- Short description (20–30 words)
- Key benefit
- Explore button

---

# Service Content

Every card must read from the service registry.

Do not duplicate data.

Each card should use:

- Name
- Slug
- Platform
- Short description
- URL
- CTA

Button:

Explore Service

Destination:

Service detail page

Example:

`/instagram/buy-instagram-followers`

---

# Design Rules

- Equal card heights.
- Consistent spacing.
- Platform badge displayed at the top.
- Primary action clearly visible.
- Minimal visual clutter.

---

# Internal Linking

Each card links directly to its service page.

Platform badges may optionally link to platform hubs.

---

# SEO

Use descriptive anchor text.

Do not repeat keywords unnaturally.

Allow search engines to discover deep commercial pages.

---

# AI Search

Descriptions should answer:

- What the service is.
- Who it is for.
- Why someone might choose it.

Keep descriptions concise and factual.

---

# Accessibility

- Entire card should remain keyboard accessible.
- Buttons require descriptive labels.
- Platform badge should not rely on color alone.

---

# Animation

Entrance:

Fade up with stagger.

Hover:

Soft lift and subtle shadow.

---

# Performance

No heavy graphics.

Icons only where useful.

All data should be rendered efficiently.

---

# Analytics

Track:

- Service card impressions.
- Explore button clicks.
- Most selected services.
- Platform-to-service conversion rate.

---

# Component Architecture

Create:

- FeaturedServicesSection
- ServicesGrid
- ServiceCard
- PlatformBadge
- ServiceCTA

Reuse existing design system components.

---

# Data Sources

Read from:

- `data/services.ts`
- `data/platforms.ts`
- `data/content/homepage.ts`

Never hardcode services inside components.

---

# Acceptance Checklist

- Responsive grid.
- Data-driven cards.
- Accessible interactions.
- SEO-friendly links.
- Consistent spacing.
- No duplicated service data.
- Matches Homepage Blueprint.
- Matches Brand Strategy.
- Production build passes.
