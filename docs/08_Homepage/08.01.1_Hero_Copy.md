# Purpose

The Hero copy introduces InstantViral in a clear, trustworthy and helpful way.

The objective is not to aggressively sell.

The objective is to help visitors immediately understand:

• What InstantViral is

• Which platforms are supported

• Why users should trust the platform

• Where to go next

---

# Accessibility Notes

The H1 must remain one heading.

CTA labels should remain short.

Trust badges must include text, not icons alone.

---

# SEO Notes

Primary keyword should appear naturally in the paragraph.

Avoid keyword stuffing.

Write for people first.

---

# AI Overview Notes

The supporting paragraph should answer:

What is InstantViral?

Who is it for?

What platforms are supported?
