# Document 07.5 — Brand Strategy (Merged v1)

This merged edition consolidates the four Brand Strategy parts into a single reference. It is the authoritative guide for design, SEO, content, copywriting and development.

## Brand Foundation

- **Mission:** Help creators and businesses grow through reliable, transparent services.
- **Vision:** Become a trusted educational-first social media growth platform.
- **Values:** Transparency, Simplicity, Trust, Customer Success, Continuous Improvement.

## Market Positioning

Compete on trust, education, clarity and UX rather than price or hype.

## Target Audience

Creators, small businesses, personal brands, agencies and researchers. Content should satisfy commercial, informational and navigational intent.

## Brand Voice

Professional, calm, helpful, honest and confident. Avoid hype, clickbait and unrealistic claims.

## Vocabulary

- **Prefer:** growth, audience, creators, quality, support, secure
- **Avoid:** guaranteed, hack, fake, bot, magic, explode, cheap

## Messaging

Explain before persuading. Answer: What is it? Who is it for? Why it matters? Why InstantViral? What next?

## Trust & EEAT

Show expertise through accurate explanations, transparency, policies, support and structured content.

## AI Search

Answer questions early, use descriptive headings, concise summaries, FAQ-ready writing and structured data.

## Homepage Rules

Guide users to the right platform, reduce confusion, build trust, then invite action.

## Service Page Rules

Hero → Benefits → Features → Process → Reviews → FAQ → Related Services → CTA.

## Learn Hub

Teach first, sell second. Link naturally to related services.

## Governance

All future content must follow this document. Consistency is mandatory.

## Implementation

- `config/brand.ts` — single source of truth
- `types/brand.ts` — strongly typed interfaces
- `lib/brand/` — helpers + validation
- Do not generate marketing copy automatically. Keep all content data-driven.
