# Document 16.06 — Newsletter Generator

Version: 1.0
Status: Production Specification

## Purpose

Generate newsletter-ready content from published Learn Center articles.

## Objectives

- Reuse article content efficiently
- Maintain consistent branding
- Encourage readers to visit the full article
- Support email campaigns

## Outputs

- newsletter.md
- newsletter.json

## Content Sections

- Subject line options
- Preview text
- Intro paragraph
- Main summary
- Key takeaways
- Primary CTA
- Optional secondary CTA

## Rules

- Reflect the published article accurately
- Do not exaggerate claims
- Keep summaries concise
- Maintain brand voice
- Support A/B subject testing

## Required Functions

- generateNewsletter()
- generateSubjectLines()
- generatePreviewText()
- validateNewsletter()

## Validation

Check:
- Missing subject
- Missing CTA
- Excessive length
- Duplicate subject options
- Invalid destination URL

## Acceptance Checklist

- Newsletter generated
- JSON validated
- Subject options created
- Preview text included
- Ready for email platform integration
