## Core Rules

- Do not state that every purchase is refundable.
- Do not promise refunds regardless of circumstances.
- Refill coverage must come only from the real package data.
- Refund decisions must follow the actual operational policy.
- Link to the Contact page for refund requests.

---

## Refund Eligibility

Explain that eligible refunds depend on:

- Order status
- Service type
- Package terms
- Operational review
- Customer compliance with instructions

---

## Non-Refundable Examples

Examples may include:

- Completed eligible orders
- Incorrect usernames or URLs supplied by the customer
- Customer changes after processing begins
- Violations of the Terms & Conditions
- Abuse or fraud

Only include situations that match the real business policy.

---

## Partial Refunds

Describe when a partial refund may be considered, if supported by the business process.

---

## Refill Policy

State that only eligible packages include refill protection.

Display refill duration and conditions from the real package data.

Do not invent refill periods.

---

## Refund Requests

Customers should provide:

- Order ID
- Checkout email
- Description of the issue
