# Document 13.02 — Contact Us Page (Production)

## Route
/contact

## Purpose
Provide customers with clear ways to contact InstantViral for sales, support, order questions, and general enquiries.

## SEO

Title:
Contact InstantViral Canada | Customer Support

Meta Description:
Contact InstantViral for sales, support, order enquiries, and general questions. Reach our team through the official contact form and support channels.

Canonical:
https://instantviral.ca/contact

1. Hero
2. Contact Options
3. Contact Form
4. Business Information
5. FAQ Preview
6. Final CTA

- Sales
- Customer Support
- Order Enquiries
- General Questions

## Contact Form

Fields:
- Full Name
- Email Address
- Subject
- Order ID (optional)
- Message

Validation:
- Required fields
- Valid email
- Message minimum 20 characters

Do not invent addresses, phone numbers, or office hours.
