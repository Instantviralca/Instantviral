## Core Rules

- Do not invent legal entity names.
- Do not invent addresses or governing jurisdiction.
- Do not promise uninterrupted service.
- Do not guarantee social media outcomes.
- Do not state that purchasing services guarantees rankings, monetization, engagement, or revenue.
- Link to the Refund Policy where appropriate.
- Use configurable values for business identity and contact information.

---

## Orders

Explain that orders are processed using the information provided by the customer and that incorrect usernames or URLs may delay or prevent fulfilment.

State that customers are responsible for verifying submitted information before payment.

---

## Pricing

Prices displayed on the website are subject to change until an order is successfully placed.

Only real package pricing from the pricing system should be displayed.

---

## Payments

Display only payment methods that are actually enabled.

Never mention unsupported payment providers.

---

## Refunds

Reference the Refund Policy.

Do not duplicate the entire refund policy.

Do not imply every order is automatically refundable.

---

## Third-Party Platforms

Clarify that Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube, and other third-party platforms operate independently from InstantViral and have their own terms and policies.
