## Learn Articles

Structure:
- Hero
- Introduction
- Table of Contents
- Main Content
- FAQs
- Related Services
- Related Articles
- CTA

## Internal Linking Rules

Homepage → Services
Services → Learn
Learn → Services
FAQ → Services
Articles → Related Articles

## Content Rules

- Helpful first
- Short paragraphs
- Clear H2/H3 hierarchy
- One search intent per page
- Avoid keyword stuffing
- Support EEAT with transparent, factual claims
