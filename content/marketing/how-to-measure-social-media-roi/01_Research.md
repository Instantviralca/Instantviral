# Research — How to Measure Social Media ROI

## Core question
How can teams measure social media ROI without fake precision?

## Short answer (planning)
Define the outcome first (leads, sales assist, support deflection), assign attribution rules you can defend, and separate leading indicators from lagging revenue. Avoid invented ROI percentages.
