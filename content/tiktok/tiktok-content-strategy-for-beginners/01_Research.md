# Research — TikTok Content Strategy for Beginners

## Core question
How should beginners plan TikTok content?

## Short answer (planning)
Beginners should pick a niche, learn hooks, and publish a small repeatable set of formats. Focus on learning loops rather than viral lottery thinking.
