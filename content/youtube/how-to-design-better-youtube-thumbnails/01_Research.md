# Research — How to Design Better YouTube Thumbnails

## Core question
What makes YouTube thumbnails effective without deception?

## Short answer (planning)
Effective thumbnails are readable at small sizes, emotionally clear, and consistent with the video. Avoid misleading imagery that spikes CTR then tanks retention.
